Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information is collected when you use Duckstreet and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Duckstreet is a simple game. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information like your name or email to play.

Google AdSense & Cookies

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google is a third-party vendor and uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies (like the DoubleClick cookie) enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings page.

For more information on how Google collects and uses data, we are required to provide this link: "How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps".

Local Storage

To improve your experience, this site uses your browser's "Local Storage" to save your game progress. This includes your saved ducks in the "Pond," your discovered items in the "Gallery," and whether you've seen the welcome popups. This data is stored *only* on your computer and is not sent to us.
