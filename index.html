Welcome to Duckstreet: The Fashion Duck Collector Game!

This is a fun, pixel art duck collecting game where you can customize your duck with rare and unique accessories. Watch ducks march by on Duckstreet, click them to steal their accessories, and build your perfect fashionable duck! Save your best creations to My Pond, and track your rare finds in the Gallery.

Ducks march by on the street, and if they have an accessory, you can click them to take it! Each accessory type (hat, wings, beak, etc.) can only have one item at a time. Send your duck to the pond to clear your current look and start collecting again!

Welcome to Duckstreet!

Duckstreet is a unique pixel art game where you can collect, customize, and save your very own virtual ducks. Watch as a parade of ducks skate down the street, each with their own unique style and accessories.

See a hat, a cool pair of sunglasses, or even some wild pants you like? Click the skating duck to claim its accessory for your own duck! Mix and match different items from various categories to create the ultimate look. When you'vecreated a masterpiece, send your duck to the Pond to save it to your personal collection.

How to Play

  • Watch the parade of ducks skate down Duckstreet. Some of them will be wearing cool accessories!
  • See an item you like? Click that duck to steal its accessory for your own duck. Your duck can only wear one of each type (e.g., one hat, one beak) at a time.
  • When you'vecreated the perfect look, click "Send to Pond" to save it to your personal collection. This will also reset your duck so you can start creating a new style!
  • Visit the "Gallery" to see all the accessories you'vediscovered and check their rarity.
  • Visit "My Pond" to view, name, and manage all the ducks you'vesaved.

About Duckstreet

Duckstreet is a loveletter to all of the idle web games I grew up playing, but I always felt they either got way too complicated or didn't offer enough variety. The pixel-art style also holds a dear place in my heart and I think is a core part of what gives Duckstreet its charm.

My goal was to create a game that was simple and relaxing to its core, but still had that feeling of infinite possibilities. I wanted you to be able to check in, see what new items you could find, and build a collection that felt truly unique!

And, if I'm being honest.... I just thought the idea of ducks on skateboards stealing each other's hats was really funny. I hope you have as much fun collecting them as I did making them!

- With Love, Matt Kingfish
